About us

St mawaggalli trades traing training istitute is a mission based skills training istitution managed by the catholic brothers of charity under the diocese of monze.

The institute is located in choma in the heart of southern province and is 1km away from choma CBD.it is named after a it's patron saint who was a ugandan martry and was later taken over by goverment in 2003 who later changed it's name to choma trades training institute.
in 2017 the christian brothers reclaimed the institute which is now called St mawaggalli trades training institute management board

The institute is accredited to technical trainig and vocational Agency (TEVETA) Which is also it's examining board and fully registered to the Engineering institution of zambia(EIZ).
The school offers a wide range of engineering and skills programs with the highest level of professional qualification that it awards being a craft certificate (2years).The lowest grade being a Trade test level 3 which is an open entry course

The courses offered include Mechanical fitting, Machinig, Power electrical, Automotive mechanics, Automotive electronics, Carpentry, Bricklayering and plastery, Information communication and technology, Metal fabrication, Tailoring, Food production and Driving.
